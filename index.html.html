What I Do

Comprehensive structural and planning solutions for every project

Building Planning and Sanction

Strategic building planning that optimizes space utilization, flow, and functionality while adhering to zoning regulations and building codes and sanction of plans.

Structural Design

Complete structural analysis and design for buildings, bridges, and special structures using advanced engineering software and methodologies.

Structural Assessment

Detailed structural inspections and assessments for existing buildings to evaluate safety, integrity, and compliance with current codes.

Sustainable Design

Eco-friendly structural solutions that minimize environmental impact while maximizing energy efficiency and long-term sustainability.

Advanced seismic analysis and design to ensure earthquake-resistant structures that protect lives and property during seismic events.

Project Consulting
